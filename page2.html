The early years

Born a Janner, Rod escaped Devon but largely stayed in the English South West. Despite odd episodes in places like Doncaster and Gosport, a significant slice was in Bath & North East Somerset. Now there's a county name to play with.

2

Employment

Largely based in Bristol, Rod rose in quite a few professions: retail, wholesale and technical representative; Information Technology, Charity and Consultancy.

3

Currently

Elected as County Councillor, Rod sits on scrutiny committees, chairs the Public Service Board Overview & Scrutiny Panel, is a member of the South Wales Fire & Rescue Authority and Branch Treasurer for Unite the Union. He has set up volunteer schemes and a local charity and through that two independent youth clubs. He is a company director and adivisor to some local schemes.
